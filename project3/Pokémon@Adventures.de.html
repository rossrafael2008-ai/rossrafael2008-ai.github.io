Liko and his Partner Floragato

Liko is a curious and kind-hearted young trainer who shares a deep bond with her partner Pokémon, Floragato. Floragato, a graceful feline-like creature with shimmering fur and psychic powers, is not only Liko’s companion but also her protector. Together, they explore the mysteries of the Pokémon world, uncovering ancient secrets and forging strong friendships. Rory is a bold and determined trainer whose strength is matched only by his loyalty. His partner Pokémon, Crocalor, is a fierce and rugged creature with a wild spirit and a powerful roar. Together, they face challenges head-on, never backing down from a battle. Crocalor's tough exterior hides a deep bond with Rory, forged through countless adventures and shared victories. Dot and her Pokémons Quaxwell and Tinkatuff 

Dot is a clever and tech-savvy trainer who prefers to stay behind the scenes, but her heart is always with her Pokémon. Her partner Quaxwell is a sleek, water-type Pokémon with a mysterious aura and unmatched agility. Alongside them is Tinkatuff, a cheerful and energetic fairy-type Pokémon who brings light and laughter wherever she goes. Friedel and Captain Pikachu

Friede is a fearless and resourceful Pokémon professor who travels the skies aboard the Brave Asagi airship. At his side is Cap, a unique Pikachu who wears a stylish captain’s hat and packs a powerful punch. Unlike most Pikachu, Cap specializes in aerial combat, often launching electrifying attacks from above. Together, Friede and Cap lead the Rising Volt Tacklers, a team dedicated to uncovering the mysteries of ancient Pokémon and protecting those in need. Their bond is built on trust, courage, and a shared love for adventure.

Amethio and his Ceruledge

Amethio is a mysterious and relentless enforcer of the Explorers, known for his sharp intellect and unwavering focus. His partner Pokémon, Ceruledge, is a spectral warrior clad in blazing purple armor, wielding twin blades of ghostly fire. Together, they strike with precision and intensity, often appearing in pursuit of ancient secrets and powerful relics. Ceruledge mirrors Amethio’s cold determination, making them a formidable duo whose presence commands respect—and fear—wherever they go. Surprisingly, Amethio is also a member of the Volt Tacklers, adding another layer of intrigue to his already enigmatic role in the unfolding journey.

Orla, Murdock, Molli, Ludlow and his Pokémons 

The Rising Volt Tacklers are a diverse crew, each member bringing their own unique strengths to the team. Orla, the brilliant engineer, works alongside her powerful Metagross to keep the Brave Asagi airship running smoothly. Murdock, the warm-hearted chef, is always accompanied by his loyal Rockruff, who adds a playful touch to every meal. Mollie, the team’s medic, relies on her gentle Chansey to care for both Pokémon and people alike. And then there’s Ludlow, the quiet and mysterious elder whose wisdom often guides the crew through uncertain skies. Together, they form a close-knit family, united by adventure and a shared mission to uncover the secrets of the Pokémon world.
